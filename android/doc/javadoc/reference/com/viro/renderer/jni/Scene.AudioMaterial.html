Class Overview

AudioMaterial is used to define the Scene's reverb effects. These are used in conjunction with setSoundRoom(ViroContext, Vector, AudioMaterial, AudioMaterial, AudioMaterial). In general, harder surface materials are more reverberant than rooms made of soft, absorbent materials.
